  <details><summary>Odin Automation and APS Standard (click to expand)</summary>
  <p>

----

  [CloudBlue](https://cloudblue.com) (called Odin Automation in 2016-2018, Parallels Automation in 2008-2016, SWsoft PEM in 2003-2008, Plesk Enterprise Manager in 2000-2003) is the world's leading e-commerce and automation platform for cloud service providers (>130 telecom companies and >80K resellers) which enables distribution of cloud services to SMBs (>30M subscriptions).

  [APS Standard](https://apsstandard.org) (version 2.0 in 2013, version 1.2 in 2010, version 1.0 in 2007) is a proposed standard for distributing, integrating, offering and provisioning cloud services (including Microsoft Office 365, Google Apps, Dropbox and >200 other services) by e-commerce and automation systems of service providers using catalogs of [integrations](http://dev.apsstandard.org/apps) and [products](https://catalog.cloudblue.com/products).

  Odin and APS Standard were [acquired](https://mi.ingrammicro.com/NewsAndEvents/Pages/News1222015.aspx) for $163.9 mln in 2015 by [Ingram Micro](http://ingrammicrocloud.com) - the world's largest IT distributor.

  Odin and APS Standard are [core parts](https://ingrammicrocloud.com/press-releases/ingram-micro-announces-the-formation-cloudblue) of [CloudBlue](https://cloudblue.com) - automation platform for distributors selling services to resellers and for providers selling services to SMB organizations.

  APS Standard is core part of [Ingram Micro Cloud Connect](https://ingrammicrocloud.com/connect) and [Cloud Blue Connect](https://cloudblue.com/connect) - automation platforms for vendors supplying services to distributors, providers and other partners of Ingram Micro Cloud.

  [AWS Marketplace](https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/saas) - a competitor to Ingram Micro Cloud Marketplace as channel for SaaS  
  [Google Orbitera](https://orbitera.com) - a competitor to Odin Automation as e-commerce platform for SaaS  

  [article](http://diversity.net.nz/ingram-micro-wants-to-help-service-providers-avoid-the-most-ridiculous-barriers-to-selling-cloud/2018/01/24) on Ingram Micro Cloud Platform (Odin Automation) and APS Standard from number 1 reporter in enterprise software
